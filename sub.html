WHY OUTSOURCE?

Customers First

Yes you! At HillSouth, we take pride in placing our customers before every aspect of our business. We understand that you just want your technology to work for you, not against you, and there is no concern for the "brand" of equipment that we use to do it. We get it, and it is because of that understanding that our entire company has been built from the ground up to offer you the constant, proactive care that you need to see your business grow. So, how do we do it?

A True Understanding

We know you like you know you

Right from the beginning of our sales process, we are striving to get to know your business. Even though we have experience in many industries, you are not a faceless statistic. Your pains are unique because they are yours. HillSouth will study your technology situation and engineer sound solutions that will drive real results for your business throughout our partnership.

TELL US ABOUT YOU

Optimization Plan

From start to finish, your in good hands

True performance comes in the form of a long-term relationship, where solutions are provided, nurtured and monitored for continued success. We break each of the years of your contract into their natural quarters. The first quarter is the on-boarding process where we implement your Stabilization Project followed by a Deep Dive Assessment where we strive to learn the absolute most about your network. The following three are monitoring quarters each separated by mini assessments and another Deep Dive Assessment.

WANT TO GET STARTED?

Service Ticket Workflow

Don't look now, but your problem has been solved

Not knowing is painful, especially when it is affecting your business. HillSouth is on your side, so we use email, online chat and phone calls as methods for you to submit your trouble tickets. However, our best method for ticket submission is our ConnectWise Customer Portal, where you can submit your ticket, see all of the notes that the technicians add, and be notified when the ticket is marked as complete. Stop worrying about your trouble tickets and worry about the things that matter, your customers.

SOLVE YOUR PROBLEMS

THE HelpDesk Solution

'THE' is in all caps because it is that good

Great customer service come from knowing two things: 1) Single points of failure are how trouble tickets get lost and ultimately ignored and 2) Put your best assets, the employees that the customers know first, as your most trusted line of defense. That is where the HillSouth HelpDesk comes in. Our Tier 1 Support solves about 80% of all trouble tickets within 30 - 60 minutes. Our Tier 2 Support has a guaranteed response time of 12 hours and acts as the face of the company. Our Tier 3 Support builds and services the most complex of problems.

LET US HELP YOU TODAY

How can we help you?

That's a great question, and it gets answered by giving us a call or sending us an email. We'll come in and take a look at your unique situation and work towards designing, building and delivering you a sound solution backed by our guarantee: to never stop thinking.
